> Def. (Kernel of a Homomorphism): Let $\varphi: G \to H$ be a group homomorphism. The *kernel* of $\varphi$ is the set: $$\ker{\varphi}\coloneqq \{g \in G: \varphi(g) = 1_{H}\}.$$
