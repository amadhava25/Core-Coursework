> Thm. (Second or *Diamond* Isomorphism Theorem): Let $G$ be a group, $A, B \leq G$, and $A \leq N_{G}(B)$. Then $AB$ is a subgroup of $G$, $B \unlhd AB, A \cap B \unlhd A$, and $AB/B \cong A / A \cap B$.
