> Def. (Cyclic Group): A group $H$ is *cyclic* if $H$ can be generated by a single element; i.e., there is some element $x \in H$ such that $H = \{x^{n}: n \in \mathbb{Z}\}$. Then, we say that $H$ is *generated* by $x$ and write $H =\braket{x}$.
